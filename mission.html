Our Mission

Our Journey with Bitblade: Pioneering Innovation in 2022

Cryptonodes.io was founded in 2022 by Bitblade, a forward-thinking German company group with a relentless focus on driving innovation in the technology realm. With a remarkable legacy in the Blockchain business since 2018, our journey has been fueled by a passion for unraveling the potential of blockchain nodes.

Introducing Cryptonodes.io: Empowering Your Blockchain Dreams

As a testament to our commitment to inclusivity and widespread access to top-notch blockchain technology, we unveiled our groundbreaking solution - Cryptonodes.io. With a firm belief that our expert skills should benefit a broader audience, we are thrilled to offer dedicated Blockchain Nodes as a Service, tailored to meet your specific needs.

Empowering Your Node, Your Way

At Cryptonodes.io, we believe in empowering our clients to make the most of their nodes. By leveraging our platform, you gain access to wallet functions that are often elusive with other node hosts. Unleash the true potential of your blockchain projects with ease, all through our user-friendly dashboard that simplifies importing and exporting keystores.

Mastering the Art of Blockchain Nodes

Throughout our extensive experience, we have honed our expertise in setting up, maintaining, and securing nodes across a diverse array of popular blockchains. Our unwavering commitment to excellence has primarily been exemplified in the proficient management of nodes for our renowned Blockchain API, Chaingateway.io.

Unmatched Security and Performance: One Node, One Server

Unlike other Node Hosts, we pride ourselves on our distinctive approach - deploying just one node per server. This exclusive practice ensures that our clients' nodes remain entirely separate from others, fostering an environment of impeccable security and unhindered performance. Whether it's a Tron Node, Ethereum Node, or Binance Smart Chain Node, you can trust us to deliver excellence without compromise.

From our founding days with Bitblade to the present, Cryptonodes.io remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation and transforming the blockchain landscape. Answers to Common Queries

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What is a dedicated blockchain node, and why do I need one?

A dedicated blockchain node is a node just for you. No one else has access to it and you don't have to share your node resources with other users. This allows a lot of power and security.

What blockchain networks are supported by your node service?

We currently support Tron, Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain.

How can I pay for my dedicated node?

You can pay with credit card using Stripe. With our payment partner Forumpay it is possible to pay with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum and Tron as well.

Is there a trial period or demo available to test your node service?

There is no trial period for our dedicated Blockchain nodes. But once you register, you have free access to our shared nodes to test latency, performance and reliability.

Can I monitor and analyze the performance of my dedicated node?

Within our Dashboard you can see key metrics like uptime, requests per second and hardware usage of your Blockchain node.

What security measures are in place to protect my data?

To access your node, you need your personal API key. Keystore files are protected and encrypted. As there is only one Node per server, only you can access it.

How reliable are the dedicated nodes in terms of uptime?

We are happy to provide an average uptime of 99.95%. In case of required maintenance we will notify you in time.
